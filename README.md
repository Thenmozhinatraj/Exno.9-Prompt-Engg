## üéØ **Aim:**

To perform the exploration of various **prompting techniques** for generating videos through AI models and to understand how descriptive, cinematic, and stylistic prompts affect the output quality.

---

## üí° **Algorithm: AI Video Prompt Engineering Framework**

1. **Select & Scope the Tools**
   Research and select suitable AI video generation tools (e.g., **Runway ML**, **Pika Labs**, **Synthesia**, **Kaiber**).
   Identify their **video length**, **motion control**, and **visual fidelity** features.

2. **Define the Core Action (Base Prompt)**
   Begin with a minimal prompt that defines the **subject** and **core movement** to evaluate how the AI interprets a simple idea.

3. **Add Environmental & Sensory Elements**
   Expand the prompt with **lighting**, **color schemes**, **ambience**, and **textures** to increase visual richness.

4. **Incorporate Cinematic Techniques**
   Include **camera movement**, **shot type**, and **motion timing** (e.g., dolly zoom, slow pan, timelapse) to direct how the scene unfolds.

5. **Determine Style & Genre**
   Define the **art style**, **mood**, or **genre** ‚Äî such as anime, noir, sci-fi, watercolor, or 3D realistic ‚Äî for creative consistency.

6. **Refine through Iteration**
   Examine the generated output and rewrite prompts to adjust unwanted aspects (speed, lighting, realism, or emotion intensity).

7. **Experiment with Variations**
   Reframe the same concept using different artistic tones, moods, or storytelling styles for comparative analysis.

8. **Document & Evaluate**
   Store and compare prompt-performance pairs to form a **prompt knowledge base** for future projects.

### Step 2: Define Core Action (Baseline Prompt)

**Example Prompt 1:**

> ‚ÄúA lion walking across a desert.‚Äù

Used in Runway ML to test basic motion understanding and subject rendering.

---

### Step 3: Add Environmental & Sensory Details

**Example Prompt 2:**

> ‚ÄúA **majestic golden lion** walking slowly across the **sunset-lit desert dunes**, with **dust swirling** around its paws and a **warm orange glow** in the sky.‚Äù

This adds environment (desert dunes), lighting (sunset glow), and sensory detail (dust movement).

---

### Step 4: Incorporate Cinematic Techniques

**Example Prompt 3:**

> ‚ÄúA **slow-motion side tracking shot** of a golden lion walking through glowing desert dunes, with a **close-up** as it looks toward the horizon.‚Äù

This guides the model to focus on camera direction and emotion.

---

### Step 5: Determine Style & Genre

**Example Prompt 4:**

> ‚ÄúA golden lion walking through glowing desert dunes. **4K cinematic nature documentary style, ultra-realistic textures, soft depth of field, shot on RED camera.**‚Äù

Here, style keywords set realism, camera type, and documentary tone.

---

### Step 6: Refine through Iteration

**Refined Prompt Example:**

> ‚ÄúA **3-second cinematic loop** of a majestic golden lion standing on a sand dune under a **crimson sunset sky**, **minimal dust**, **smooth motion**, filmed in **photorealistic 8K**.‚Äù

The refinement improves smoothness, realism, and environment balance.
